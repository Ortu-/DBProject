Matrix1 Utility Plug-ins

Matrix1Util_01

This plug-in provides an alternative to DBPro's memblock system. You can't do everything with them that you can do with memblocks (images, objects, sounds etc), but you can have more of them and manipulate them more easily. They also provide a nice easy way to read/write files too.
