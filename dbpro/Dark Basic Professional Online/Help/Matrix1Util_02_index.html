Matrix1 Utility Plug-ins

Matrix1Util_02

This plug-in's primary purpose is to provide timers that will 'tick' for you. These allow you to trigger regular events in a very simple manner.

It also provides hi-resolution timers for you to use whenever a high level of accuracy is needed.
Finally, it also allows you to increase the resolution of the standard DBPro TIMER() function.
